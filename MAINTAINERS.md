## Becoming a maintainer

We welcome new maintainers. Candidates are typically community members who:

- Have made meaningful, sustained contributions over several months
- Are familiar with the codebase, the OVH API, and CAPI conventions
- Demonstrate good judgement in code reviews and issue triage

To express interest, please reach out to the current maintainers or open an
issue.

## Escalation

For project-related questions or escalations, please open an issue or
discussion on GitHub. For security-sensitive matters, see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
